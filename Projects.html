Our Projects

We are proud to have worked on numerous impactful geospatial projects, helping our clients leverage the power of spatial data to make informed decisions. Below is a showcase of some of our most exciting work.

Assessment of Spatial Morphology of Contagion index in Port Harcourt Metropolis

This Project involved the Examination of the spatial morphology of the study area from the road network, the pattern of building volume across the study area, also the relationship between spatial morphological indices, population density and building volume across the study area.

Radiation exposure Analysis

We provided geospatial analysis of public exposure from radiation in six local governments in Edo State by performing Normalization of datasets and interpolations for Average Dose rate, Average exposure rate, ELCR and AEDE.

Remote Sensing and land use land cover map classification in Port Harcourt

This project aimed to Analyse and produce Land use land cover map classification (LULC) of study area as well as Normalized difference Vegetation Index (NDVI).
